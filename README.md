> “The more I learn, the more I realize I don’t know.”

Tobiloba builds and maintains secure, resilient systems that protect critical infrastructure and data. He approaches every project by tracing how information flows, where it could fail, and how it can be recovered. His work spans network and software security, DevOps, application deployment, observability, and monitoring, ensuring that services run smoothly and issues are caught before they affect users. He writes backend and automation code in Go, relies on thorough testing to confirm behaviour, and uses metrics to guide decisions rather than guesswork.

He learned his craft on physical routers and switches, which taught him to think in terms of packets and failure modes. That background informs his security practices: permissions stay tight, logs remain clear, and controls stay active even under tight deadlines. In cloud environments, he focuses on predictable scaling, clear separation of risk, and actionable insights when something drifts out of line.

Outside of work, he contributes to open-source email security tooling under Apprentice Systems, using Go to simplify how developers implement and verify authentication protocols.
